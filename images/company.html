Comprehensive offerings of transceivers compatible with international standards.

Our PSTN Telecom Testers/Analyzers

Our CATV – Test Meters & Test Instruments

GAO Tek’s handheld CATV/ QAM analyzers are designed for both digital and analog TV analysis. They have digital signal analysis functions for channel power, MER, BER and constellation diagrams for 16/32/64/128/256QAM/QPSK modulation types. Analog channel analysis includes single-frequency level, carrier level of full channels spectrum, HUM, modulation depth, TILT and C/N.
